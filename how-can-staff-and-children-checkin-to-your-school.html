    How can Staff & Children Check-in to your school?

    Easy checkin for staff and children

    Staff Checkin
    1. For staff checkin staff need to login through their MyKidReports mobile app (Download for Android or iPhone).
    2. Now open burger menu at the top left corner and click on Check-in Kisosk, your camera will open (make sure allow your camera permission when asked), now scan the QR code given by school. Staff is successfully checked-in. Follow the same to checkout.

    Children Checkin
    1. For children checkin parents need to login through their MyKidReports mobile app.
    2. Parents will see list of child if you have multiple chilren in same school, now click on check In button associated with them.
