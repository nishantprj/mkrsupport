How to edit checkin hours for staff and children

Easy checkin for staff and children

  1. Open side menu by clicking that "Burger icon" at top left corner of your mobile and click on Check In Reports.
  2. Now you can access report for every child and teacher.
  1. Select child or teacher to view their report.
  2. Click on time (Check-in or Check-out) and set desired time and click "OK". Check-in time updated successfully.
