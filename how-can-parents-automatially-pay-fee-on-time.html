    How can Parents Automatially Pay Fee on Time?

    Parent can easily make online and automatic payments by following the steps below:

    Step 1: Connect with Caredit Card or Direct Bank Debit
    1. Parents need to login to theirMyKidReports account and proceed to "Payment Settings" in payments associated with each child.
    2. Now parents need to select accordingly if they want to connect with Credit Card or with Direct Bank Debit.

    Step 2: Verify Connection Status
    1. After completed the setup, parents will be able to see the status of connection. They need to look for the "Connected" status to confirm that everything is working smoothly.
    2. If connected with both Credit Card and Dirent Bank Debit, parents can select which one they'd like to set as default.

    By following the setps above parents are ready for automatic paymets, their trasaction will be automatically processed on billing date set by school.
