How can staff see their hours worked for a given week?

Setup your Center add rooms, staff, children and manage activities you do in your school

Step 1: Create Rooms for Your School
  1. Imagine your school as a big puzzle, and rooms are the pieces. Click on "Create Rooms" and enter room name and save.
  2. Your room is now created, you can always change room name and room image if you want to.
Step 2: Add Staff Members
  1. To add staff members to your preschool, click on "Add Staff."
  2. A popup will appear where you can enter the name and email of the staff member. Click "Save" to add the staff member to your preschool.
  3. Staff member added sucessfully. You can assign a room to them and edit their profile at any time.
Step 3: Manage Activities you do in your Prechool
  1. Activities play a crucial role in providing parents with daily updates on what their child is doing in school.
  2. Mykidreports offers default activities for your school, which you can customize to fit your needs.
  3. Additionally, you can create new activities to suit your preschool's unique curriculum.
Step 4: Add Children

There are two convenient methods for adding children to your preschool.

Method 1 (Upload Child Roster):
  1. Easily add multiple children at once. Click on "Upload Child Roster" and follow the provided instructions to upload your list of children.
Method 2 (Add One by One):
  1. To add children individually, click on "Add Child."
  2. A popup window will open, where you can input the required details of the child. Make sure to include parent information for better communication between parents and teachers.
  3. Click "Save" to add the child to your preschool.


Congratulations! You've successfully set up your preschool on Mykidreports. With these steps completed, you're well on your way to efficiently managing your preschool's operations, staff, activities, and parent communication.
