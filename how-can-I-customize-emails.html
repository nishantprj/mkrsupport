    How can I customize emails?

    Why you need to customize your emails?

    Emails not only create a stronger connection with my recipients but also enhance my brand recognition and professionalism. Customization enables you to convey specific information, instructions, and updates effectively, making my emails more relevant and engaging.

    Step 1: Go to settings and customize your email
    1. Go to "messaging and emails" in settings
    2. Select the specific email you want to customize from the list (e.g., Parent Welcome Email, Teacher Daily Reports Email).
    3. Add your own words, personalize the message, and make any necessary changes to suit your school's style and preferences. Save your customized email.
