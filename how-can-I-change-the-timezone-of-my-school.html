    How can I change the timezone of my school?

    Easily change timezone according to your location.

    Why time zone is important?

    At MyKidReports we understand that every preschool operates in its unique geographical location. Customizing your time zone on MyKidReports offers a significant advantage for your preschool. It ensures that all scheduling, event notifications, and data records are aligned with your specific location, leading to accurate and reliable operations. This customization facilitates seamless communication with parents and staff, eliminating any timing-related confusion and enhancing the overall user experience.

    Step 1: Go to Settings and change Timzone
    1. Navigate to "Profile" in settings.
    2. Look for the "Time Zone". Edit and select your desired time zone from the dropdown.
