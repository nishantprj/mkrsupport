Easy checkin for staff and children

Staff Checkin
  1. For staff checkin staff need to login through their Mykidreports mobile app.
  2. Now open burger menu at the top left corner and click on Check-in Kisosk, allow you camera permission if required and scan the QR code given by school. Staff is successfully checked-in. Follow the same to checkout
Children Checkin
  1. For children checkin parents need to login through their Mykidreports mobile app.
  2. You will see list of child if you have multiple chilren in that school, now click on checkin button associated with them.
