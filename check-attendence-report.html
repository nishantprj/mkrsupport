    Easy checkin for staff and children

    Why you need reports?

    Reports provide you valuable data for optimizing scheduling and resource allocation, leading to increased efficiency. Moreover, they facilitate transparent communication with parents about their child's daily activities and attendance, fostering trust and engagement.

    On Computer
    1. If you are on computer, just login to MyKidReports and you can see reports on side menu.
    2. Click on it and you can access multiple reports, for now click on "Check-in" reports to view attendence of teachers and children.

    On Mobile
    1. If you are on mobile (Download for Android or iPhone) just login to it and open side menu by clicking that "Burger icon" at top left corner of your mobile.
    2. Once menu opened you can see "Check-in Reports", click on it and view attendence to teachers and children.
